About Graston Medical Clinics

عيادات جراسستون للعلاج الطبيعي (Graston Medical Clinics) is a network of specialized physical therapy and rehabilitation centers dedicated to providing innovative, evidence-based treatment for patients recovering from injuries, surgeries, or managing chronic conditions.

Our clinics feature state-of-the-art equipment and are staffed by highly trained physical therapists, rehabilitation specialists, and support staff committed to delivering personalized care and achieving optimal outcomes for each patient.

Our Specialized Services

Graston Technique

Our signature therapy using specialized instruments to effectively treat soft tissue restrictions and break down scar tissue, promoting faster healing and improved mobility.

Sports Rehabilitation

Specialized programs for athletes at all levels focusing on injury recovery, performance enhancement, and preventive strategies to minimize future injuries.

Orthopedic Therapy

Comprehensive treatment for orthopedic conditions affecting joints, muscles, and bones, including post-surgical rehabilitation and management of chronic conditions.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy for patients recovering from stroke, traumatic brain injury, spinal cord injury, and other neurological conditions to improve function and quality of life.

Functional Rehabilitation

Goal-oriented therapy focusing on restoring the ability to perform daily activities and returning patients to their pre-injury lifestyle with confidence.

Pain Management

Comprehensive programs combining various therapeutic techniques to manage chronic pain, reduce medication dependence, and improve overall function and quality of life.

Our Therapeutic Approach

Patient-Centered Care

At Graston Medical Clinics, we believe in a holistic, patient-centered approach to rehabilitation. Our treatment plans are tailored to each individual's specific needs, considering their medical history, lifestyle, and personal goals.

Personalized Treatment

Progress Monitoring

Patient Education

Empowering patients with knowledge and self-management techniques for long-term success
